Certificate of Completion

Awarded for successfully completing a Marketplace Simulation. Read more about the criteria for this Open Badge.


Issued by: Marketplace Simulations on August 18, 2023

HOW TO CLAIM THIS BADGE

  • Create a badge backpack.

    Make a free account on a service (like Badgr) with your preferred email address. This is the backpack where you can collect this and other Open Badges you earn.

  • Add any other email addresses necessary to the backpack account.

    The email you used to play Marketplace is the cryptographic key to validate this badge. If this email is not the same as the email as the email you chose for your backpack account, add the Marketplace email as a secondary email to the Badgr account.

  • Use one of the options here to claim your badge in the backpack.

    From the backpack, import the Marketplace badge into your account using one of the three options below. You will then be able to save it and easily share it.
    • Save the unique, encoded image for this awarded badge.
    • Copy the permalink for this awarded badge.
    • Download the JSON code for this awarded badge.
